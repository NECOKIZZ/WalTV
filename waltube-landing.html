How it works

Three steps to owning your content forever.

1

Upload your AI content

Publish videos or AI-generated media to WalTube. Your content is stored permanently on Walrus — decentralized and immutable from day one.

2

Set your royalty terms

Define what happens when someone forks or remixes your work. Publish the terms onchain — enforcement is automatic, no middlemen needed.

3

Earn forever

Every fork, every paid like, every remix generates income — transparently, automatically, directly to your wallet. No expiry. No platform cuts.
